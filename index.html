ABOUT

"There's nothing you can't do." A saying that I've heard throughout my life, growing up as a nerdy, tri-racial, spanish-speaking kid in K-Town Los Angeles, and I believed every word of it. I attribute this saying with motivating me towards many successes, but there is no greater feeling of accomplishment for me than being a Web Developer in the SF Bay Area, creating something from beginning to end that helps others and can be seen and appreciated. Finding this path, which holds an endless journey of excitement and challenges, has forced me, and the teams I have worked with, to think creatively and produce greatness. I eventually found my family's saying to be flawed. With the help of others, not only could I go further in my life, but together we can create amazing things. While some things never change and I will always be the same nerdy guy, I now live my life by the saying "There's nothing WE can't do", and I believe it! So how about it? Let's make something great.
